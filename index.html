The Significance of Friday

Best Day of the Week

Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) said: "The best day on which the sun has risen is Friday; on it Adam was created, on it he was admitted to Paradise, and on it he was expelled from it." (Sahih Muslim)

Hour of Acceptance

There is a special hour on Friday during which Allah grants the supplications of His servants. This hour is sought after by believers for their duas to be accepted.

Obligatory Congregation

Jumu'ah prayer is obligatory for every adult Muslim male. It consists of two rak'ahs preceded by a sermon (Khutbah) which provides spiritual guidance.
